How do I get started?

SkillSet first understands your background. You answer a specially created form including your history, what you want to learn and your intended level of mastery.

AI tag and search

The platform relies on a powerful deep neural network trained on millions of resumes and job requirements. This creates search tags from the filled form and it is also used to rank content quality.

Video Curation

SkillSet relies on open video data sources such as YouTube and its own community driven video sharing service to search through and curate a learning curriculum for any skill.
